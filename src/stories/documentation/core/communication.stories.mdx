<Title>Communication</Title>
<Subtitle>Confirmation and acknowledgement communications ask for confirmation before taking an action and acknowledge successful actions.</Subtitle>

### Types of actions
<ul>
<li><strong>Confirming actions</strong> ask the user to verify that they want to proceed with an action.</li>
<li><strong>Acknowledging actions</strong> provide text to let the user know whether an action they chose has been completed.</li>
</ul>

### Acknowledgement components
<p>
  Acknowledgements can be delivered by a variety of components. Criteria for choosing the right acknowledgement component includes:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Urgency level</li>
<li>Inclusion of an action to correct a problem</li>
<li>Duration on screen (transient, dismissable, or both)</li>
</ul>
<p>Acknowledgements that are transient mean the component will exit on its own within a few seconds of appearing. Dismissable acknowledgements can be dismissed by selecting an action to dismiss the component.</p>

<Title>Colours</Title>
<Subtitle>At Qasir, colors play an important role when we are working with a product, a service or a space. The right combination of colors can influence how users feel, think, and behave.</Subtitle>

### Principles

<ul>
    <li>
        <b>Hierarchical</b>
        <p>Color indicates which elements are interactive, how they relate to other elements, and their level of prominence. Important elements should stand out the most.</p>
    </li>
    <li>
        <b>Legible</b>
        <p>Text and important elements, like icons, should meet legibility standards when appearing on colored backgrounds.</p>
    </li>
    <li>
        <b>Expressive</b>
        <p>Show brand colors at memorable moments that reinforce your brandâ€™s style.</p>
    </li>
</ul>
